Naruto is a popular Japanese anime and manga series created by Masashi Kishimoto. It follows the story of Naruto Uzumaki, a young ninja with dreams of becoming the strongest leader, known as the Hokage, of his village. Despite being an outcast due to the powerful Nine-Tails Fox sealed within him, Naruto strives to gain the respect of others and protect his friends. The series explores themes of friendship, perseverance, and overcoming personal challenges while showcasing intense action-packed battles and deep character development.
